About Me

I'm Happy To Help With Your Next Project

Hi, I’m Melissa M. Buie — the heart and hands behind The Coding Phoenix.

As the founder of The Coding Phoenix, I specialize in designing, revamping, and streamlining websites for purpose-driven entrepreneurs, ministries, and small business owners. Whether you're just starting out or looking to breathe new life into your brand, I’m here to bring your vision to life — with clarity, creativity, and care.

Why “The Coding Phoenix”? Because just like the phoenix rises from the ashes, I believe your business can be reborn with the right digital presence. Every website I create is infused with strategy, heart, and faith — helping you build with confidence and purpose.

I’m not just here to deliver a pretty site. I’m here to walk alongside you, support your goals, and help you build something that lasts. Let’s build something meaningful — together.
